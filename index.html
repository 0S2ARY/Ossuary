Ossuary

In the year 3029, Axis Elliot Ward lives an ordinary life in a Dystopian world scorched by an unstable sun. That life ends the day he walks into his house to find his mother and a monstrous intruder. Axis, rebuilt with expensive cybernetics, is forced into debt. As he heads into the underground, believing that his life was over, he has a revelation. Axis joins a worldwide animal hunting association known as the GraveDiggers. Believing it's the only way to save his life. Hunting the supernatural fauna of his world, Axis slowly loses himself, making a habit out of his job. .
